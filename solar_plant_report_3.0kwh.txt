#### 1. Complete Step-by-Step Installation Process
- **Initial Consultation and Site Survey**: Engage with a solar energy consultant to evaluate your energy needs and site conditions. The consultant will assess the roof structure (for rooftop installations) or land (for ground-mounted systems), shading, and orientation towards the sun.

- **Permitting**: Submit the system design to local authorities to obtain necessary building and electrical permits. This may include utility approval for grid connection.
- **Procurement**: Purchase the solar panels, inverter, mounting system, and other electrical components based on the design specifications.
- **Installation**:
  - Install mounting system securely, ensuring it's properly aligned and anchored.
  - Mount the solar panels onto the mounting structure.
  - Install the solar inverter, typically near the main panel.
  - Implement electrical wiring to connect solar panels, inverter, and the grid.
  - Install safety disconnects and grounding system.
- **Inspection and Commissioning**: Have a certified electrician or inspector review the installation to ensure compliance with local codes. Following approval, the system can be commissioned and connected to the grid.
- **System Monitoring Setup**: Install monitoring software to track the system's performance and energy production.
